Cristiano Ronaldo

The GOAL Machine

Here's a timeline of Ronaldo's life.

  • 1985- Born in Madiera Portugal.
  • 1997- Aged 12 joined Sporting CP youth academy.
  • 2002- Made his debut for Sporting CP and scored 2 goals.
  • 2003- He joined Manchester United.
  • 2008- Won his 1st Ballon d'Or.
  • 2009- Joined Real Madrid as the most expensive player in the world.
  • 2013- Won his 2nd Ballon d'Or. Also won La Decima(the tenth) UCL for Real Madrid.
  • 2014- Won his 3rd Ballon d'Or.
  • 2015- Became Real Madrid all time leading goal scorer.
  • 2016- Won his 4th Ballon d'Or.
  • 2017- Won his 5th Ballon d'Or.
  • 2018- Left Real Madrid after 9 seasons to join Juventus in Seria A.

“When you play with Ronaldo on your team, you are already 1-0 up.” -- Zinedine Zidane
